[If game is lagging or does not work properly on Google Chrome]
Try switching "Use if hardware acceleration is available" from Settings-> Advanced-> System
Or try another browser such as Microsoft Edge

[Control]
A：Move to the left
D：Move to the right
SPACE：Jump
Left click：Shoot to mouse cursor
Mouse wheel：Weapon change
R：Game start and Retry
